Key Features and Benefits

Let’s break down why these upper control arms make a real difference:

Adjustable Alignment

These control arms come with adjustable ball joints, giving you up to +/-2 degrees of camber and 0 to +4 degrees of caster adjustment. That means no more struggling with alignment after lifting your truck.

High-Angle Ball Joints

Designed for lifted trucks, the high-angle ball joints allow for greater range of motion. This means better off-road articulation and fewer limitations when you hit uneven terrain.

Heavy-Duty Construction

Built with durability in mind, SPC control arms are forged from strong, corrosion-resistant materials. They’re tough enough to take on off-road abuse, harsh weather, and daily driving.

Maintenance-Friendly

Each ball joint is greasable and replaceable, so you’re not locked into a single-use part. That makes long-term maintenance simple and cost-effective.

OEM Fitment

Installation is straightforward because these arms are built to OE dimensions. No cutting, welding, or guessing just bolt them in and go.
